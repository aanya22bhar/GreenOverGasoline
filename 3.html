Plaine Products Environmentally Friendly Shampoo and Conditioners

Plaine Products is a sister-owned company, founded by Lindsey and Alison Delaplaine, that specializes in vegan, cruelty-free, palm oil free, chemical-free and biodegradable hair and natural body wash products. Since the entire company started with the dream of having less plastic in the world, their entire production model is completely plastic free.
